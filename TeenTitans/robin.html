ROBIN

Robin is the leader and founder of the Teen Titans. He currently resides in Go City.
Robin, born as Dick Grayson, was born to Mary and John Grayson.
All three of them form an acrobatic group known as the 'The Flying Graysons'.
Unfortunately, this came to an end when Robin's parents tragically fell to the deaths during an act.
He was later brought up by Batman and became his sidekick. When Robin became older, he set out to fight crime on his own.
